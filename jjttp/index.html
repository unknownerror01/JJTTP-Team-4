    Tour Packages

    We offer a variety of hand selected packages that will give the vacation you've deserved. Whether this is your first vacation as a family, couple or alone, we've got the perfect vacation for you're searching for.

    • Guided Trips

      First time jitters? Take a tour with one of our experts. They'll show you secrets that you're likely to miss otherwise.

    • Photo Trips

      Want to experience all these tropical beauties without actually breaking a sweat? Take a photo tour on one of our air conditioned helicopters.

    • Horseback Riding

      Never rode a horse by the beach? Consider taking a ridealong trail through one of our beach shore paths. We'll provide the equipment, and lunch too!

    • Racing Trips

      Got a competitive spirit? Sign up for one of our atv races! Try to reach the finish line on the trail we have set out before anyone else and win a special prize.
