    Tour Packages

    We offer a variety of hand selected packages that will give you the vacation you deserve. Whether this is your first summer vacation as a family, couple or alone, we've got the perfect vacation just for you.

    • Guided Trips

      First time? Take a tour with one of our experts. They'll show you the secrets that you're likely to miss otherwise.

    • Photo Trips

      Want to experience all the tropical beauties without actually breaking a sweat? Take a photo tour on one of our air-conditioned helicopters.
